Hi, I’m Jeff.

I’m a designer, developer, and writer based in San Francisco.

I’m currently a Design Manager at Optimizely. Before becoming a manager I was a Product Designer and a UI Engineer.

Before joining Optimizely, I earned my Master’s degree at UC Berkeley’s School of Information, where I studied human-computer interaction, usability, and behavioral economics.

I’m also a tennis player and musician (guitar/bass/drums).
