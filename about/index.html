Hi, I’m Jeff.

I’m a humane designer & technologist who’s passionate about building technology to improve the world.

I’m the former Head of Design at Optimizely. Before that I was a Product Designer and a UI Engineer.

I’m also a tennis player and musician (guitar/bass/drums).
